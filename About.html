I can't really remember a time where I wasn't wanting to do two things: make games and tell stories. As a kid, I loved to read and to analyze books, I'd read just about any book I could get my hands, and play twice that number of games.
I'm a firm believer that games are a still untapped well of artistic potential. With a good book, you're transported to a different world. With a movie, you get to see that world. Through a song, you can experience emotion in a universal language. But in games, you can be the one experincing them for yourself, you become the character, and it's that unique experience that I believe puts games at the top of artistic expression.
