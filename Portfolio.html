Interactive Mobile Comic and Game

This is a project that me and a group of other students were tasked with creating during our Freshman year. We were given a high concept topic to explain in the form of a interactive comic. My group also included a small platformer game in which the player changes perspectives to complete the level. It was created with Unity and a plugin called Panoply for the comic portion. I was in charge of managing the project, coming up with the visual narrative devices, storyline, the general concept of the game.
